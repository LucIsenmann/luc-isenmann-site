Luc Isenmann

Biographie (FR)

Luc Isenmann, batteur jazz formé au CNSM de Paris et au CRR de Strasbourg, est une figure du jazz contemporain. Élève de Daniel Humair, il développe une carrière de sideman à Paris depuis 1997, jouant dans de nombreux groupes réguliers ou occasionnels.

Il a collaboré avec de nombreux musiciens français tels que Gary Brunton (Pee Bee et Brunt’Off), David Patrois/Jean-Charles Richard Trio, Antoine Delaunay/Mélanie Dahan 5tet, Michel El Malem, Benjamin Moussay Trio, Géraldine Laurent/Laurent de Wilde 4tet, Fred Favarel Trio et Pierre de Bethmann.

Il joue également aux côtés de musiciens européens ou américains tels que Greg Osby 4tet, Marc Copland, Jerry Bergonzi, Jacky Terrasson, Ricky Ford et Sarah Morrow.

Luc Isenmann enseigne la batterie jazz au Conservatoire de Strasbourg.

Biography (EN)

Luc Isenmann is a jazz drummer trained at the CNSM in Paris and the CRR in Strasbourg, and a prominent figure in the contemporary jazz scene. A former student of Daniel Humair, he has been active as a sideman in Paris since 1997, performing in both regular and occasional ensembles.

He has collaborated with many French musicians including Gary Brunton (Pee Bee, Brunt’Off), David Patrois/Jean-Charles Richard Trio, Antoine Delaunay/Mélanie Dahan Quintet, Michel El Malem, Benjamin Moussay Trio, Géraldine Laurent/Laurent de Wilde Quartet, Fred Favarel Trio, and Pierre de Bethmann.

His musical journey has also led him to work with European and American artists such as Greg Osby Quartet, Marc Copland, Jerry Bergonzi, Jacky Terrasson, Ricky Ford, and Sarah Morrow.

In addition to his performance career, Luc Isenmann teaches jazz drums at the Strasbourg Conservatory.
