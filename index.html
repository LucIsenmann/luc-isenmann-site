Batteur de jazz contemporain

Trained at the Paris CNSM and Strasbourg CRR, Luc Isenmann is a figure of contemporary jazz. A student of Daniel Humair, he has been an active sideman on the Paris scene since 1997, playing in many regular and occasional groups.
